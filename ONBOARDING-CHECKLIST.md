# Employee Onboarding Checklist: Book Digitization Platform

Welcome to the team! This checklist is designed to ensure a seamless transition into our AI-driven document digitization ecosystem. Our goal is to move you from "new hire" to "impactful contributor" with clarity, support, and excitement.

---

## 🗓️ PHASE 1: PRE-START (The Logistics)
*Goal: Remove all administrative friction before the first day.*

- [ ] **Offer & Legal:**
    - [ ] Signed Offer Letter received.
    - [ ] Background check completed and cleared.
    - [ ] Signed NDA and Intellectual Property (IP) agreements.
- [ ] **Equipment Order:**
    - [ ] Ship hardware (Laptop, Monitor, Peripherals) to arrive 2 days before start date.
    - [ ] Confirm delivery address and contact info.
- [ ] **Account Provisioning:**
    - [ ] Create company email address.
    - [ ] Provision access to core systems (see *Systems Access Checklist*).
    - [ ] Add to relevant Slack channels.
- [ ] **Welcome Email:**
    - [ ] Send "What to Expect" email (Start time, location/link, first-day schedule, dress code, and what to bring).
    - [ ] Introduce the "Onboarding Buddy."

---

## 🗓️ PHASE 2: DAY 1 (The Welcome)
*Goal: Connection, culture, and basic setup.*

- [ ] **Orientation:**
    - [ ] HR/Admin session: Benefits, payroll, and company handbook.
    - [ ] **Company Overview Presentation:** Mission (Digitizing the world's knowledge), Vision (AI-first document intelligence), and our core technology stack.
- [ ] **Workspace Setup:**
    - [ ] Hardware unboxing and workstation ergonomics check.
    - [ ] Login verification for all primary systems.
- [ ] **Team Introductions:**
    - [ ] Formal welcome by the Manager.
    - [ ] Team "Meet & Greet" (Quick intros to immediate teammates).
    - [ ] **Buddy Assignment:** Meet your assigned Onboarding Buddy for "unwritten rule" questions and social support.
- [ ] **Tool Access Verification:**
    - [ ] Confirm access to: **Slack, Notion, GitHub, Jira, Google Workspace, and HRIS.**

---

## 🗓️ PHASE 3: WEEK 1 (The Integration)
*Goal: Understanding how we work and what we build.*

- [ ] **Alignment:**
    - [ ] **1:1 with Manager:** Discuss immediate expectations, communication styles, and preferred feedback loops.
    - [ ] **Team Norms Review:** Review the "Team Ways of Working" doc (Async vs. Sync communication, meeting cadences, "Deep Work" hours).
- [ ] **Product & Tech Immersion:**
    - [ ] **Product Walkthrough:** End-to-end demo of the Digitization Platform (from raw scan to AI-processed structured data).
    - [ ] **Codebase/Workflow Walkthrough:** (For Engineers) Architecture overview, CI/CD pipelines, and AI model deployment workflows.
- [ ] **The "Quick Win":**
    - [ ] Assign and complete one **small, low-stakes task** (e.g., a documentation update, a small bug fix, or a minor UI tweak) to build momentum.
- [ ] **Social Integration:**
    - [ ] **Daily Lunch Rotation:** Scheduled lunches with different team members each day (e.g., Mon: Engineering, Tue: Product, Wed: AI Research, Thu: Sales/Ops, Fri: Casual/Social).

---

## 🗓️ PHASE 4: MONTH 1 (The Foundation)
*Goal: Building competence and cross-functional context.*

- [ ] **Skill Building:**
    - [ ] Complete all mandatory **Training Modules** (Security Awareness, AI Ethics & Bias, Data Privacy/GDPR).
    - [ ] Deep-dive training on our specific OCR and LLM orchestration layers.
- [ ] **Cross-Functional Connection:**
    - [ ] Scheduled "Coffee Chats" with leads from adjacent departments (e.g., if in Engineering, meet with Product Management).
- [ ] **Contribution:**
    - [ ] Completion of the **First Meaningful Deliverable** (e.g., a feature component, a research summary, or a customer success playbook).
- [ ] **Review:**
    - [ ] **30-Day Check-in:** Formal meeting with Manager to discuss: *What is going well? Where are the blockers? Is the role what you expected?*

---

## 🎯 30-60-90 DAY GOALS TEMPLATE
*To be co-created by Manager and Employee during Week 1.*

| Milestone | **30 DAYS: LEARNING** | **60 DAYS: CONTRIBUTING** | **90 DAYS: LEADING** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Focus** | Absorbing context, tools, and culture. | Owning tasks and improving processes. | Driving initiatives and autonomy. |
| **Goal 1** | Understand the full digitization pipeline. | Complete [Project X] independently. | Propose a new optimization for [Process Y]. |
| **Goal 2** | Master all internal tools and workflows. | Collaborate with [Team Z] on a shared goal. | Mentor a new hire or lead a sprint/meeting. |

## 📋 ONBOARDING FEEDBACK SURVEY
*To be sent at the end of Month 1.*

1.  **Clarity:** On a scale of 1-5, how clearly were your initial responsibilities defined?
2.  **Tools:** Did you have all the hardware and software access you needed to be productive?
3.  **Culture:** Do you feel welcomed and integrated into the team culture?
4.  **Buddy:** How helpful was your Onboarding Buddy in your first two weeks?
5.  **Gaps:** What is one thing we could have done differently to make your first month better?
6.  **Open Feedback:** Anything else you’d like to share?
