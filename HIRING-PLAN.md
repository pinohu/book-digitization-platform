### 2. COMPENSATION BENCHMARKING

**Methodology:**
*   **Levels.fyi:** Used for US-based technical roles (CTO/AI Engineer) to ensure competitiveness in the Silicon Valley/Remote US market.
*   **Pave:** Used for real-time startup compensation data and equity modeling.
*   **Carta:** Used to benchmark equity percentages against similar seed-stage AI startups.
*   **Local Market Data:** For Nigeria-based roles, we use local benchmarks (e.g., Glassdoor Nigeria/local recruiters) to ensure "fair but optimized" pay.

**Geographic Adjustments:**
*   **Core Tech (US/Remote):** High cash/high equity.
*   **Operations (Nigeria):** Localized cash (competitive for Nigeria) / low equity.
*   **AI/ML (Global/Remote):** Competitive USD-denominated salaries to attract top talent from Eastern Europe/India/US.

**Cash vs. Equity Tradeoff:**
*   **Early Stage (Founders/First 3):** High Equity / Lower Cash. They are building the value.
*   **Growth Stage (Hires 4-5):** Balanced Cash / Moderate Equity. They are scaling the value.

### 7. REMOTE HIRING STRATEGY

**Where to find candidates:**
1.  **LinkedIn:** Best for US-based leadership and Sales.
2.  **Wellfound (formerly AngelList):** Best for early-stage startup enthusiasts.
3.  **Upwork/Toptal:** Best for specialized, short-term technical or AI tasks.
4.  **GitHub/StackOverflow:** Best for finding niche AI/ML talent.
5.  **Local Nigerian Tech Communities (e.g., Andela, local hubs):** Best for Operations and local scaling.

**Screening for Remote Readiness:**
*   **Communication Test:** Ask for a written summary of a technical concept (tests written clarity).
*   **Infrastructure Check:** Verify stable internet and power (crucial for Nigeria-based roles).
*   **Time-Zone Trial:** Conduct a trial interview at an "off-hour" to test flexibility.
*   **Asynchronous Test:** Assign a 24-hour task to see how they manage work without real-time guidance.

**Onboarding Remote Employees:**
*   **Digital HQ:** Use Notion for documentation and Slack for "watercooler" talk.
*   **Over-Communication:** Weekly video 1-on-1s are non-negotiable.
*   **Visibility:** Use project management tools (Linear/Jira) so everyone sees progress without asking.
