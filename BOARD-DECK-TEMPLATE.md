# Board Deck Template — Book Digitization Platform

This template is designed for a **20-minute high-impact presentation**. To achieve this, the presenter should spend roughly 1.5–2 minutes per slide, reserving the final 5 minutes for discussion and voting.

---

# Board Deck Template: [Company Name] Q[X] [Year]

## Slide 1: Agenda & Key Decisions
*Goal: Set expectations and frame the meeting.*
* **Data to Include:** 
    * Meeting agenda items.
    * A "Decision Log" section: List the 2–3 specific items that require a formal Board vote today.
* **Visualization Type:** Clean, structured bulleted list with a "Decision Required" call-out box.
* **Talking Points:** "Today we will review our performance against our AI roadmap and financial targets. Most importantly, we need your approval on [Decision A] and [Decision B] by the end of this session."

## Slide 2: Executive Summary
*Goal: The "TL;DR" for the entire quarter.*
* **Data to Include:** 
    * **Win:** (e.g., "Achieved 99.2% OCR accuracy on handwritten manuscripts.")
    * **Challenge:** (e.g., "Sales cycle for enterprise libraries is longer than projected.")
    * **Focus:** (e.g., "Scaling GPU infrastructure for the new LLM-integration.")
* **Visualization Type:** Three distinct high-contrast cards/boxes.
* **Talking Points:** "The quarter was defined by technical breakthroughs in semantic understanding, though we are navigating a slower enterprise sales cycle. Our primary focus moving forward is compute efficiency."

## Slide 3: Financial Overview
*Goal: Demonstrate fiscal health and sustainability.*
* **Data to Include:** 
    * Total Revenue (ARR/MRR).
    * Net Burn (Monthly).
    * Cash on Hand & Runway (in months).
    * Actual vs. Budget variance.
* **Visualization Type:** 
    * Bar chart for Revenue growth.
    * Line chart for Burn vs. Cash Balance.
    * Large "Big Number" call-out for **Runway**.
* **Talking Points:** "Revenue is tracking 10% ahead of budget. Our burn remains controlled, giving us [X] months of runway, providing sufficient time to reach our next milestone."

## Slide 4: KPI Dashboard
*Goal: Show operational efficiency and product efficacy.*
* **Data to Include:** 
    * **Product Metric:** Digitization Accuracy % & Pages Processed per month.
    * **Efficiency Metric:** Cost per page (Compute/API costs).
    * **Growth Metric:** Net Revenue Retention (NRR).
    * **Target Variance:** Current vs. Quarterly Goal.
* **Visualization Type:** "Traffic Light" Scorecard (Green/Yellow/Red icons) next to each metric.
* **Talking Points:** "Our accuracy is at an all-time high, but our cost-per-page has ticked up slightly due to the increased complexity of the new models. We are optimizing this in Q3."

## Slide 5: Product Roadmap
*Goal: Show technical momentum and future value.*
* **Data to Include:** 
    * **Shipped (Q[X]):** (e.g., Multilingual support, API v2).
    * **Building (Q[X+1]):** (e.g., Automated layout reconstruction).
    * **Planned (Long-term):** (e.g., Real-time mobile scanning).
* **Visualization Type:** Horizontal Timeline or Kanban-style columns.
* **Talking Points:** "We successfully rolled out the semantic search feature. We are currently deep in development on layout reconstruction, which will be our primary differentiator in the next quarter."

## Slide 6: Go-To-Market (GTM) Update
*Goal: Prove market fit and sales scalability.*
* **Data to Include:** 
    * Sales Pipeline Value (Weighted vs. Unweighted).
    * Conversion Rate (Lead $\rightarrow$ MQL $\rightarrow$ Closed Won).
    * CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) trends.
    * Top 3 customer segments (e.g., Academic, Legal, Government).
* **Visualization Type:** Sales Funnel chart and a Line chart for CAC trends.
* **Talking Points:** "Our pipeline is expanding, particularly in the legal sector. While CAC has stabilized, we are seeing a higher conversion rate from our new content-marketing efforts."

## Slide 7: Team & Hiring
*Goal: Assess organizational strength and culture.*
* **Data to Include:** 
    * Total Headcount (split by Eng, Sales, Ops, G&A).
    * Key Hires made this quarter.
    * Open/Critical Roles (e.g., Head of AI, VP of Sales).
    * eNPS or turnover rate (if applicable).
* **Visualization Type:** Simple Org Chart snippet and a "Hiring Progress" bar chart.
* **Talking Points:** "We’ve successfully added two senior ML engineers. Our priority for next month is filling the VP of Sales role to accelerate our GTM efforts."

## Slide 8: Competitive Landscape
*Goal: Contextualize the company within the market.*
* **Data to Include:** 
    * Competitor feature parity (e.g., Accuracy, Speed, Integration).
    * Market shifts (e.g., "Competitor X moving from OCR to Generative AI").
    * Our "Moat" (e.g., Proprietary dataset, specialized model architecture).
* **Visualization Type:** 2x2 Matrix (e.g., "Accuracy" vs. "Ease of Integration") or a Feature Comparison Checklist.
* **Talking Points:** "While legacy players are moving into the AI space, their models lack our specific focus on complex document layouts, which remains our primary competitive advantage."

## Slide 9: Risk Register
*Goal: Show proactive management of threats.*
* **Data to Include:** 
    * Top 5 Risks (e.g., AI Regulation, Compute Cost Volatility, Talent War, Data Privacy, Competition).
    * Probability (Low/Med/High) and Impact (Low/Med/High).
    * Mitigation Strategy for each.
* **Visualization Type:** Heat Map (Impact vs. Probability).
* **Talking Points:** "Our highest risk is the rising cost of GPU compute. We are mitigating this by transitioning to more efficient, smaller-parameter models where possible."

## Slide 10: Strategic Decisions (The Vote)
*Goal: Drive action and formalize board governance.*
* **Data to Include:** 
    * **Proposal:** Clear statement of what is being decided.
    * **Context:** Why this decision is being made now.
    * **Pros/Cons:** Brief summary of the impact.
    * **Recommendation:** Management's stance.
* **Visualization Type:** Large, clear text boxes. "ACTION REQUIRED" header.
* **Talking Points:** "We are presenting Proposal A: The expansion of our credit facility. The pros include faster scaling; the cons include higher interest. Management recommends approval. I'll open the floor for discussion and a vote."

## Slide 11: Appendix
*Goal: Provide "Deep Dive" data for the curious/skeptical.*
* **Data to Include:** 
    * Detailed P&L.
    * Cohort Analysis (Retention by month).
    * Detailed Customer Case Studies.
    * Technical Architecture diagrams.
* **Visualization Type:** Data-heavy tables and granular charts.
* **Talking Points:** "Detailed financial breakdowns and cohort data are available in the appendix for your review after the meeting."
