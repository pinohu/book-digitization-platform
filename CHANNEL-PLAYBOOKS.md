# Channel Playbooks — Book Digitization Platform

This playbook is designed for **Origin Eyes Inc.** to scale the "Digitization-as-a-Service" model. Since your revenue is transactional ($5/book), your goal is **High-Volume/Low-CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**. You are not just selling a service; you are selling "Legacy Preservation" and "Digital Monetization."

---

### 1. LINKEDIN OUTBOUND (The "Legacy Builder" Sequence)
**Goal:** Connect with decision-makers at niche publishing houses, religious organizations, and academic archives.

*   **ICP Definition:**
    *   **Titles:** CEO, Founder, Head of Digital Transformation, Acquisitions Editor, Archivist, Librarian, Director of Collections.
    *   **Industries:** Publishing, Religious Institutions, Higher Education, Non-profit Organizations.
    *   **Company Size:** 1–50 employees (Niche publishers) or 500+ (Large libraries/universities).
*   **Sales Navigator Boolean Strings:**
    *   `("Publisher" OR "Publishing") AND ("Religious" OR "Catholic" OR "Academic" OR "Classical")`
    *   `("Archivist" OR "Librarian" OR "Curator") AND ("Collection" OR "Archives")`
    *   `("Founder" OR "CEO" OR "Owner") AND ("Book" OR "Textbook" OR "Print")`
*   **Connection Request (3 Variations):**
    1.  *The Peer:* "Hi [Name], noticed your work with [Company Name]. I’m working on a project to help niche publishers bridge the gap between physical archives and digital accessibility. Would love to connect."
    2.  *The Mission:* "Hi [Name], I admire the legacy of [Company Name]. I specialize in helping organizations digitize physical classics using AI. Hope to connect!"
    3.  *The Direct:* "Hi [Name], I'm building an AI platform to help publishers monetize their back-catalogs via digitization. Thought it might be relevant to your work at [Company Name]."
*   **The 21-Day Sequence:**
    *   **Day 1 (Accepted):** "Thanks for connecting, [Name]. I'm currently helping organizations like Criterion Educational Foundation turn their physical libraries into searchable digital assets. No pitch—just happy to have you in my network."
    *   **Day 3 (Value):** Share a PDF or link: *"The ROI of Digitizing Niche Book Collections in 2024."*
    *   **Day 7 (Problem Awareness):** "Quick question, [Name]: Is [Company Name] currently facing challenges with the physical storage or accessibility of your older back-catalog titles?"
    *   **Day 14 (The Pitch):** "We’ve streamlined the process to just $5/book using AI-driven scanning. We handle the heavy lifting so you can focus on distribution. Would you be open to a 10-minute chat about your backlog?"
    *   **Day 21 (Breakup):** "I assume digitization isn't a priority for [Company Name] right now. I'll leave you with this: [Link to Case Study]. If things change, I'm here."
*   **Daily Targets:** 20 New Connections, 15 Follow-ups, 1 Thought-leadership post.
*   **Benchmarks:** 25-30% Connection Rate; 10% Response Rate.
*   **Tools:** Sales Navigator, Expandi.io (for automated, human-like sequencing).

### 2. CONTENT MARKETING (The "Authority" Engine)
**Goal:** Position Origin Eyes as the expert in "Preserving Heritage through AI."

*   **Content Pillars:**
    1.  **The Preservation Mission:** The importance of saving physical history/religious texts.
    2.  **The Tech Edge:** How AI improves OCR (Optical Character Recognition) accuracy vs. traditional scanning.
    3.  **The Business Case:** How to turn "dusty books" into "passive digital income."
    4.  **Case Studies:** The "Criterion" story (The transition from physical to digital).
*   **Weekly Calendar:**
    *   **Monday (Blog):** Long-form SEO piece (e.g., *"How AI is Revolutionizing Religious Text Preservation"*).
    *   **Wednesday (LinkedIn Article):** Opinion piece (e.g., *"Why Niche Publishers are Losing Money by Ignoring Digital Back-catalogs"*).
    *   **Friday (Newsletter):** "The Digitization Digest" – Curated news on publishing tech + a "Book of the Week" feature.
*   **SEO Template:**
    *   **H1:** [Keyword-rich Title]
    *   **H2:** The Problem with [Current Method]
    *   **H2:** How [AI/Your Solution] Solves It
    *   **H2:** Step-by-Step Implementation
    *   **CTA:** "Get a free quote for your collection [Link]."
*   **90-Day Ramp Plan:**
    *   **Month 1 (Foundation):** 4 Blogs on "The Why."
    *   **Month 2 (Proof):** 4 Blogs on "The How" (Tech deep dives).
    *   **Month 3 (Conversion):** 4 Blogs on "The ROI" (Case studies/Pricing).
*   **KPIs:** Organic Traffic (Growth %), Time on Page (>2 mins), Newsletter Signups.

### 3. GOOGLE ADS (The "Intent" Capture)
**Goal:** Capture people actively searching for digitization solutions.

*   **Campaign Structure:**
    *   **Brand:** Keywords: "Origin Eyes Inc," "Origin Eyes Digitization."
    *   **Competitor:** Keywords: "[Competitor Name] alternatives," "Professional book scanning services."
    *   **Category (High Intent):** "Bulk book digitization services," "Convert physical books to ebook," "AI book scanning."
    *   **Long-tail (Niche):** "Digitize religious book archives," "How to digitize old library collections."
*   **Keyword Example:** `[book digitization service near me]`, `"convert hardcovers to PDF"`, `"bulk scanning for publishers"`.
*   **Ad Copy Template (RSA):**
    *   *Headline 1:* Professional Book Digitization
    *   *Headline 2:* Only $5 Per Book | AI-Powered
    *   *Headline 3:* Preserve Your Library's Legacy
    *   *Description:* Turn your physical back-catalog into searchable digital assets. High-accuracy AI scanning. Get a quote today!
*   **Landing Page Requirements:**
    *   **Hero:** "Your Physical Books, Digitized for $5/Book."
    *   **Social Proof:** "Trusted by Niche Publishers & Archives."
    *   **Single CTA:** "Get a Free Volume Estimate."
*   **Budget Allocation:** 40% Category, 30% Long-tail, 20% Competitor, 10% Brand.
*   **Bidding:** Start with **Manual CPC** to gather data $\rightarrow$ Move to **Target CPA** once you have 30+ conversions/month.
*   **Negative Keywords:** `free`, `jobs`, `software download`, `how to scan at home`.

### 4. EMAIL COLD OUTREACH (The "Direct ROI" Sequence)
**Goal:** High-volume outreach to small-to-mid-sized publishers.

*   **The 5-Email Sequence:**
    *   **Email 1 (The Pattern Interrupt):**
        *   *Subject:* Your back-catalog at [Company Name]
        *   *Body:* "Hi [Name], I was looking at [Company Name]'s collection. You have incredible titles, but I noticed they aren't available in digital formats. We help publishers digitize their entire backlog for just $5/book. Interested in seeing how it works?"
    *   **Email 2 (The Proof):**
        *   *Subject:* Re: Your back-catalog at [Company Name]
        *   *Body:* "Just following up. We recently helped a foundation digitize their classics, making them accessible globally. Here is a sample of the OCR accuracy: [Link/Image]."
    *   **Email 3 (The Pain Point):**
        *   *Subject:* The cost of physical storage
        *   *Body:* "Hi [Name], physical books take up space and degrade over time. Digital copies last forever and sell 24/7. Can we chat about a pilot program for 50 of your books?"
    *   **Email 4 (The Comparison):**
        *   *Subject:* Traditional scanning vs. AI-driven
        *   *Body:* "Most scanning services charge $20+ per book. Our AI-driven model keeps it at $5. You get higher accuracy at a fraction of the cost. [Link to comparison table]."
    *   **Email 5 (The Breakup):**
        *   *Subject:* Moving this off my desk
        *   *Body:* "I haven't heard back, so I'll assume digitization isn't a priority. If you ever want to unlock the value in your physical archives, you can find me here. Best, [Name]."
*   **Subject Line Formulas:** `[Company Name] + [Benefit]`, `Question regarding [Niche]`, `Quick idea for [Company Name]`.
*   **Deliverability:** Setup **SPF, DKIM, and DMARC** via Google Workspace/Microsoft 365. Use **Instantly.ai** or **Lemlist** for warm-up.
*   **Limits:** Max 30-50 new emails/day per inbox to avoid spam filters.

### 5. PRODUCT HUNT LAUNCH (The "Visibility" Event)
**Goal:** Not to find $5 customers, but to find *partners, investors, and media.*

*   **T-30:** Reach out to "Hunters" (top PH users) to see if they'll hunt you. Gather 5 testimonials from your pilot (Criterion).
*   **T-14:** Create a "Coming Soon" landing page. Offer a "Launch Day Special" (e.g., first 100 books free for new users).
*   **T-7:** Finalize assets. Ensure your video shows the AI "magic" (scanning $\rightarrow$ text appearing).
*   **T-1:** Email your entire list. "We are launching on Product Hunt tomorrow. We need your support!"
*   **Launch Day (The Sprint):**
    *   *00:01:* Post on PH.
    *   *08:00:* Blast LinkedIn/Twitter/Email.
    *   *12:00:* Respond to every single comment on PH.
    *   *18:00:* Update supporters on your rank.
*   **Post-Launch:** Send a "Thank You" email to everyone who engaged. Write a blog post: *"What we learned launching Origin Eyes on Product Hunt."*
*   **Asset Specs:**
    *   Thumbnail: 240x240 (Clean, high-contrast logo).
    *   Gallery: 1270x760 (Show the "Before" physical book and "After" digital interface).
    *   Video: <2min (The "Problem $\rightarrow$ Solution $\rightarrow$ Result" arc).
