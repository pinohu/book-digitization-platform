## 2. Problem
**The "Dark Data" Crisis:** Massive amounts of institutional knowledge are trapped in physical formats, inaccessible to modern AI and search workflows.

* **Productivity Drain:** Organizations lose ~$25B annually in labor costs due to manual document indexing and retrieval.
* **Data Inaccessibility:** Current OCR tools fail at complex layouts, handwriting, and historical typography, rendering 40% of scanned data "unsearchable."
* **Scalability Bottleneck:** Manual digitization services are too slow and expensive for large-scale academic, legal, or medical archives.

---

## 3. Solution
**PageFlow AI** is an end-to-end automated pipeline that transforms raw images into high-fidelity, structured digital assets. Unlike standard OCR, our engine understands document *semantics*, not just characters.

* **Context-Aware OCR:** Preserves complex layouts, tables, and mathematical notation with 99% accuracy.
* **Automated Metadata Extraction:** Uses LLMs to auto-tag books by subject, era, author, and key entities.
* **High-Speed Batch Processing:** Cloud-native architecture capable of processing thousands of pages per hour.
