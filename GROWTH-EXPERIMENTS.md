# Growth Experiments — Book Digitization Platform

This is a high-impact mission. Because your price point is low ($5/book), you are playing a **volume game**. Your Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) must be extremely low, and your viral coefficient must be high to achieve sustainability.

Here is your Growth Hacking Roadmap.

---

### AWARENESS (Top of Funnel)
*Goal: Introduce the concept of "Digital Preservation" to religious and academic institutions.*

1.  **Content Marketing Hack: "The Lost Classics Series"**
    *   **Hypothesis:** Sharing snippets of rare, digitized Catholic texts on social media will attract scholars and clergy.
    *   **Implementation:** Create 60-second "Micro-History" Reels/TikToks featuring a beautiful page from a classic, its history, and a "See the full digital version" CTA.
    *   **Cost:** Low (Content creator/Social media manager).
    *   **Timeline:** Ongoing (2x weekly).
    *   **Success Metric:** Organic reach/Shares.
    *   **Target:** 10k views/month.

2.  **Partnership Play: The Seminary Outreach**
    *   **Hypothesis:** Partnering with Catholic seminaries and theological colleges will provide a massive, concentrated user base.
    *   **Implementation:** Offer a "Institutional Pilot Program" where you digitize their 10 most fragile books for free in exchange for a featured spot in their newsletter.
    *   **Cost:** Low (Cost of processing 10 books).
    *   **Timeline:** 1 month.
    *   **Success Metric:** Number of institutional leads.
    *   **Target:** 5 Seminary partnerships in Q1.

3.  **Community-Driven Growth: The "Archive Guardians" WhatsApp Group**
    *   **Hypothesis:** Creating a community for librarians and religious archivists in Nigeria will create a feedback loop and organic word-of-mouth.
    *   **Implementation:** Launch a WhatsApp/Telegram group for "Preservation Experts" where you share tips on book care and AI tech updates.
    *   **Cost:** $0.
    *   **Timeline:** 2 weeks.
    *   **Success Metric:** Group member growth rate.
    *   **Target:** 200 active members.

4.  **Unconventional PR Stunt: "The Digital Resurrection"**
    *   **Hypothesis:** A high-profile "rescue" of a decaying, famous text will garner news coverage.
    *   **Implementation:** Identify one highly significant, physically crumbling Catholic text in Nigeria. Publicize a "Save the Text" campaign to digitize it before it's lost forever.
    *   **Cost:** Medium (Marketing/PR outreach).
    *   **Timeline:** 2 months.
    *   **Success Metric:** Media mentions/Press pickups.
    *   **Target:** 3 local/religious news features.

---

### ACQUISITION (Lead Gen)
*Goal: Convert interest into a "Digitization Project" lead.*

1.  **Landing Page Optimization: The "Heritage" Angle**
    *   **Hypothesis:** A landing page focused on "Preserving Sacred Legacy" will convert better than one focused on "AI Tech."
    *   **Implementation:** A/B test "Fast AI Digitization" vs. "Protect Your Faith's History."
    *   **Expected CAC:** $0.50 (Organic/Search).

2.  **Lead Magnet: The "Digitization Readiness" Audit**
    *   **Hypothesis:** Offering a free PDF checklist on "How to prepare fragile books for scanning" will capture emails of librarians.
    *   **Implementation:** A gated PDF download on the site.
    *   **Expected CAC:** $0.80 (via targeted FB Ads).

3.  **Channel Arbitrage: Facebook Group Targeting**
    *   **Hypothesis:** Highly specific targeting of "Catholic Theology" and "History of Nigeria" interests on Meta is cheaper than Google Search.
    *   **Implementation:** Run "Problem/Solution" ads: "Don't let your library crumble. Digitizing classics for $5/book."
    *   **Expected CAC:** $1.20.

4.  **Paid Creative Test: The "Before/After" Video**
    *   **Hypothesis:** A split-screen video showing a dusty, illegible page vs. a crystal-clear, searchable digital page will drive clicks.
    *   **Implementation:** 15-second video ad test.
    *   **Expected CAC:** $1.50.

---

### ACTIVATION (The "Aha!" Moment)
*The Aha! Moment: When a user sees a high-resolution, fully searchable (OCR) digital sample of one of their own specific books.*

1.  **Onboarding Flow: The "Instant Preview"**
    *   **Implementation:** Allow users to upload 3 photos of a book page for a "Free Instant Digital Sample" to prove AI accuracy immediately.

2.  **Time-to-Value (TTV) Reduction: Auto-OCR Preview**
    *   **Implementation:** Instead of "We will email you," show the text extraction on the screen within 30 seconds of upload.

3.  **Activation Email Sequence: The "Proof of Value"**
    *   **Implementation:** Email 1: "Your sample is ready." Email 2: "See how searchable your library could be (Video)." Email 3: "Bulk discount offer."

---

### RETENTION (Repeat Business)
*Retention Period: 6 Months. Target: 20% of users ordering a second batch of books.*

1.  **Engagement Loop: The "Project Progress" Dashboard**
    *   **Implementation:** A dashboard where users see their "Library Progress" (e.g., "12/50 books digitized"). This gamifies the preservation process.

2.  **Habit Formation: The "Monthly Archive Day"**
    *   **Implementation:** A monthly email reminder: "It's Archive Day. Which 5 books are you saving this month?"

3.  **Re-engagement: The "Lost Treasure" Campaign**
    *   **Implementation:** If a user hasn't uploaded in 60 days, send: "Your library is still aging. Don't let these classics disappear."

---

### REFERRAL (Viral Loops)
*Target Viral Coefficient (K): 0.2 (Every 5 customers bring 1 new customer).*

1.  **Referral Mechanic: "The Librarian's Credit"**
    *   **Implementation:** Give $1 credit to the referrer and $1 credit to the referee for every book digitized.

2.  **Viral Loop within Product: The "Digitized By" Watermark**
    *   **Implementation:** For public-facing digital previews, include a small, elegant "Digitized by Criterion Platform" link.

3.  **Social Proof/UGC: The "Digital Shelfie"**
    *   **Implementation:** Encourage users to share a screenshot of their new digital library on LinkedIn/Twitter with a branded hashtag.

---

### REVENUE (Maximizing LTV)
*Goal: Increase Average Order Value (AOV) from $5.*

1.  **Pricing Page A/B Test: The Bundle Effect**
    *   **Implementation:** Test "$5 per book" vs. "The Scholar Bundle: 12 books for $50" (Slightly higher price, but higher AOV).
    *   **Expected Impact:** +25% AOV.

2.  **Upsell Flow: The "Enhanced Search" Add-on**
    *   **Implementation:** At checkout, offer "Advanced Semantic Search" (AI-powered topical searching) for an extra $2 per book.
    *   **Expected Impact:** +40% Revenue per transaction.

3.  **Annual Plan: The "Institutional Subscription"**
    *   **Implementation:** Offer a $500/year plan for unlimited digitization (up to 150 books) for religious schools.
    *   **Expected Impact:** Massive cash flow stability.

---

### VIRAL LOOP DESIGN
**Trigger:** A researcher finds a physical book in a library.
**$\rightarrow$ Share:** They see the "Digitize this" QR code/link on the platform and share it with a colleague.
**$\rightarrow$ Accept:** The colleague clicks and sees a free sample of a similar book.
**$\rightarrow$ Value:** The colleague realizes they can own a searchable version of their own library.
**$\rightarrow$ Trigger:** The colleague uploads their first book, starting the cycle.

**Target K-Factor:** $K = i \times c$ (where $i$ is invites sent and $c$ is conversion). Aim for **K = 0.3** to ensure steady organic growth.

---

### REFERRAL PROGRAM TEMPLATE
*   **Incentive:** "Preserve & Earn" — $1 credit for both parties per book.
*   **Mechanics:** Unique referral link generated in the User Dashboard.
*   **Tracking:** Unique ID attached to every upload via the referral link.
*   **Fraud Prevention:** Credits only applied *after* the referred user's payment is cleared; limit to 50 referrals per user to prevent botting.
*   **Terms:** Credits cannot be cashed out; they must be used for further digitization.
