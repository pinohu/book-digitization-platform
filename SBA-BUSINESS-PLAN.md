## 5. Products & Services

**Detailed Description:**
The platform is an AI-powered pipeline:
1.  **Ingestion:** High-resolution images of book pages are uploaded.
2.  **AI Processing:** OpenAI Vision models perform OCR (Optical Character Recognition), preserving complex religious formatting (footnotes, Latin marginalia).
3.  **Structuring:** The system converts raw text into structured JSON, then exports to searchable PDF and ePub formats.
4.  **Delivery:** Institutions receive a digital vault of their library, searchable by keyword, chapter, or theme.

**Pricing Model:**
*   **Transactional:** $\$5.00$ per book digitized.
*   **Bulk Discount:** $\$4.00$ per book for libraries exceeding 1,000 volumes.

**Product Lifecycle:**
*   **Phase 1 (MVP):** Basic OCR to PDF conversion.
*   **Phase 2 (Optimization):** ePub export and advanced theological metadata tagging.
*   **Phase 3 (Expansion):** AI-powered "Chat with the Library" (RAG) for scholars.

**Intellectual Property:**
*   Proprietary prompt engineering and post-processing scripts for religious text formatting.
*   Exclusive licensing agreements for the Criterion catalog.
