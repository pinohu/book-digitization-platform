# Performance Management Framework — Book Digitization Platform

This Performance Management Framework is designed specifically for the **Book Digitization Platform**. Given the unique nature of your business—bridging high-tech AI development in the US with the preservation of sacred Catholic Classics in Nigeria—this framework emphasizes **accuracy (quality), mission alignment, and cross-border operational excellence.**

---

# Performance Management Framework: Book Digitization Platform

## 1. REVIEW CADENCE
To maintain momentum in a fast-paced AI environment while ensuring the delicate nature of Catholic texts is respected, we follow a multi-layered rhythm:

*   **Weekly Standup Updates (Team Visibility):** 15–30 minute syncs. Focus on blockers, weekly throughput (e.g., pages scanned/processed), and immediate technical hurdles.
*   **Monthly 1:1 Check-ins (Employee-Driven):** Lightweight, informal meetings. Focus on "How are you doing?" rather than "What are you doing?" to prevent burnout and ensure alignment with the mission.
*   **Quarterly Performance Reviews (Full Cycle):** Deep dives into KPIs and competencies. This is the formal assessment period.
*   **Annual Compensation Review:** Conducted in January, tied directly to the Q4 performance review results.
*   **Review Timeline:**
    *   **Q1 Review:** April
    *   **Q2 Review:** July
    *   **Q3 Review:** October
    *   **Q4 Review + Annual Comp:** January
*   **Review Hierarchy:** Direct Manager (Primary) $\rightarrow$ Skip-level input (Context) $\rightarrow$ Peer Feedback (360-degree perspective).

## 2. EVALUATION CRITERIA TEMPLATE

### Core Competencies (All Roles)
1.  **Execution & Delivery:** Quality of output (low error rates in digitization), speed, and reliability in meeting deadlines.
2.  **Collaboration & Communication:** Effectiveness in a distributed (US/Nigeria) environment; clarity in documentation.
3.  **Problem-Solving & Initiative:** Ability to troubleshoot AI edge cases or logistical scanning hurdles without constant supervision.
4.  **Growth Mindset & Learning:** Speed of adopting new AI tools or liturgical/historical knowledge.
5.  **Alignment with Values:** Respect for the sanctity of the texts and the mission of Criterion Educational Foundation.

### Role-Specific Competencies
*   **AI/ML Engineering:** Model precision/recall, latency reduction, data pipeline robustness, and algorithmic efficiency.
*   **Digitization Ops (Scanning/Data Entry):** Physical handling of rare books, OCR accuracy, throughput (pages/hour), and equipment maintenance.
*   **Domain Experts (Theology/Classics):** Accuracy of transcription, contextual correctness, and ability to identify liturgical nuances.
*   **Sales/Partnerships:** New foundation onboarding, fundraise conversion, and partnership retention.

### Rating Scale & Behavioral Anchors
| Rating | Label | Behavioral Anchor |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **5** | **Exceptional** | Consistently delivers beyond scope; innovates new processes; mentors others. |
| **4** | **Exceeds** | Often delivers above targets; requires minimal supervision; proactive. |
| **3** | **Meets** | Consistently meets all requirements and core KPIs; reliable. |
| **2** | **Partially Meets** | Meets some goals but requires frequent correction or oversight. |
| **1** | **Does Not Meet** | Fails to meet basic requirements; performance issues are impacting the team. |

*Note: We use distribution guidance to ensure "Exceptional" is truly rare, preventing rating inflation.*

## 3. SELF-ASSESSMENT FORM
*To be completed by the employee 1 week prior to the Quarterly Review.*

1.  **Key Accomplishments:** List 3–5 major wins this quarter. Include measurable impact (e.g., *"Improved OCR accuracy for Latin texts by 12%"*).
2.  **Goal Status:** Review last quarter's goals. What was achieved? What was missed, and why?
3.  **Challenges:** What obstacles (technical, logistical, or personal) hindered your performance?
4.  **Growth Areas:** Where do you feel you need more training or support?
5.  **Manager Feedback:** What is your manager doing that helps you? What should they stop or start doing?
6.  **Next Quarter Goals:** Propose 3–5 SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound).
7.  **Career Interests:** What skills or roles are you interested in moving toward?
8.  **Self-Rating:** Provide a 1–5 rating for yourself with a brief justification.

---

## 4. MANAGER ASSESSMENT FORM
*To be completed by the manager following the employee's self-assessment.*

1.  **Goal Performance:** Rating (1–5) for each specific KPI assigned at the start of the quarter.
2.  **Competency Ratings:** Rating (1–5) for each core competency with written justification.
3.  **Key Strengths:** Specific examples of where the employee excelled.
4.  **Development Areas:** Actionable suggestions for improvement.
5.  **Overall Performance Rating:** Final 1–5 rating (subject to calibration).
6.  **Promotion Readiness:** [Not Ready] / [Developing] / [Ready Now].
7.  **Compensation Recommendation:** (For Annual Reviews) Based on merit bands.
8.  **Future Expectations:** Specific goals and focus areas for the upcoming quarter.

---

## 5. CALIBRATION PROCESS
*Purpose: To prevent "easy graders" and "hard graders" from creating inequity between the US and Nigeria teams.*

*   **Participants:** All managers at the same level + their skip-level manager.
*   **Preparation:** Managers submit ratings and written justifications before the meeting.
*   **Process:**
    1.  Review the distribution of ratings across the company.
    2.  Discuss "Edge Cases" (employees on the border between 3 and 4, or 2 and 3).
    3.  Adjust ratings to ensure a "4" in the Engineering team means the same as a "4" in Ops.
*   **Rules:** Discuss **behaviors and outcomes only**. No personality-based discussions.
*   **Bias Mitigation:** Explicitly check for *Recency Bias* (judging only by the last two weeks) and *Proximity Bias* (favoring those in the same time zone/office).

## 6. PERFORMANCE IMPROVEMENT PLAN (PIP) TEMPLATE
*Initiated for sustained "Does Not Meet" ratings or critical errors in data integrity.*

*   **Concerns:** Documented examples of performance gaps (e.g., *"Failed to meet transcription accuracy threshold for 3 consecutive weeks"*).
*   **Expected Standards:** Clear, measurable targets (e.g., *"Maintain 98% accuracy on all scanned pages"*).
*   **Support Provided:** Specific training, mentorship, or updated tools to be provided by the company.
*   **Milestones:** 30, 60, and 90-day checkpoints with specific review dates.
*   **Check-in Cadence:** Weekly mandatory meetings between manager and employee.
*   **Consequences:** Clear statement that failure to meet PIP milestones may lead to separation.
*   **Outcomes:** Successful Completion, Extension, Role Change, or Separation.

---

## 7. PROMOTION CRITERIA
*Promotion is based on demonstrated capability, not just tenure.*

*   **The "Next Level" Rule:** The employee must have been performing at the next level's complexity and scope for at least one full quarter.
*   **Impact:** Demonstrates impact beyond their immediate tasks (e.g., an engineer who improves the workflow for the entire scanning team).
*   **Skills Matrix:**
    *   **IC1–IC2 (Junior):** Learning tools, executing tasks with guidance.
    *   **IC3–IC4 (Mid/Senior):** Independent execution, solving complex problems, mentoring juniors.
    *   **IC5–IC6 (Staff/Principal):** Shaping technical strategy, cross-departmental influence.
    *   **M1–M3 (Management):** People development, resource allocation, strategic alignment.
*   **Promotion Packet:** Includes manager recommendation, peer endorsements, and a "portfolio of impact" (evidence of work).

---

## 8. COMPENSATION REVIEW
*Annual review tied to Q4 performance.*

*   **Market Benchmarking:** Using data from *Levels.fyi* (for Tech) and local Nigerian market data (for Ops) to ensure competitive, fair pay.
*   **Merit Increase Bands:**
    *   **Exceptional (5):** 9–12% increase
    *   **Exceeds (4):** 6–8% increase
    *   **Meets (3):** 3–5% increase
    *   **Partially Meets (2):** 0% increase
    *   **Does Not Meet (1):** 0% increase / PIP
*   **Equity Refresh:** (For US-based/Key Tech roles) Granted based on performance and retention priority.
*   **Off-Cycle Adjustments:** Only for promotions or significant market corrections.
*   **Communication:** All compensation changes are delivered via a formal "Total Rewards Statement" explaining the *why* behind the number.
