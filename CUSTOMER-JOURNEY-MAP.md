### 🔍 Deep Dive into Key Stages

#### **Stage 4: Onboarding (The Critical First 7 Days)**
*   **Day 1:** Welcome email + "Quick Start" video. Goal: Get them to upload a file.
*   **Day 2:** In-app tooltip highlighting "Auto-Tagging" feature.
*   **Day 4:** Milestone Email: *"You just saved 4 hours of manual typing! See how."* (Reinforcing value).
*   **Day 7:** Checklist check-in: *"Is your workspace set up? Need a demo?"*

#### **Stage 5: Adoption (Habit Formation)**
*   **The "Aha!" Moment:** When the user sees a perfectly formatted, searchable PDF from a messy, handwritten manuscript.
*   **Habit Loop:** User has a stack of books $\rightarrow$ Uploads to platform $\rightarrow$ Receives high-quality digital assets $\rightarrow$ Integrates into their research/workflow.

#### **Stage 6: Expansion (The Revenue Engine)**
*   **Transactional Trigger:** User reaches 90% of their credit limit $\rightarrow$ Trigger "One-click top-up" notification.
*   **SaaS Trigger:** User adds more than 3 team members $\rightarrow$ Trigger "Upgrade to Team Plan" for centralized billing and permissions.
