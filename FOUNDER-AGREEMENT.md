## 7. DEPARTURE SCENARIOS

### A. Voluntary Departure ("Good Leaver")
A Founder leaves due to health, family, or seeking new opportunities without breaching this agreement.
*   **Result:** Retains all *vested* shares. Forfeits all *unvested* shares.

### B. Involuntary Departure ("Bad Leaver")
A Founder is terminated for "Cause" (e.g., fraud, criminal activity, gross negligence, or material breach of this agreement).
*   **Result:** The Company reserves the right to buy back *all* shares (vested and unvested) at [Fair Market Value / a 50% discount to FMV].

### C. Death or Disability
*   **Death:** The Founder’s estate shall retain vested shares, subject to any buy-sell agreements in the LLC agreement. The Company may maintain "Key Person" life insurance to fund a buyback of shares from the estate.
*   **Disability:** If a Founder is unable to perform duties for [90] consecutive days, they shall be treated under the "Good Leaver" or "Bad Leaver" provisions as determined by the Board.
