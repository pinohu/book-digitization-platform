# Compensation Benchmarks — Book Digitization Platform

This compensation framework is designed for a high-growth AI startup specializing in Document Digitization. Given the "Deep Tech" nature of AI, engineering roles command a premium compared to general SaaS.

### **Data Source Integration Methodology**
*   **Levels.fyi:** Used for technical role leveling and percentile accuracy in high-cost tech hubs.
*   **Pave:** Used for real-time SaaS compensation trends and equity-to-cash ratios.
*   **Carta:** Used for equity distribution benchmarks and cap table modeling across funding stages.

### **2. Adjustment Factors**

#### **A. Geographic Multipliers (Applied to Base Salary)**
*   **San Francisco (SF):** 1.0 (Benchmark)
*   **New York City (NYC):** 0.95
*   **Remote (US-based):** 0.85
*   **Austin/Denver/Atlanta:** 0.80

#### **B. Stage Adjustments (Cash vs. Equity Trade-off)**
| Stage | Cash Strategy | Equity Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pre-Seed/Seed** | Minimize cash to extend runway. | Maximum equity to attract top talent. |
| **Series A** | Market-competitive cash for mid-level. | Significant equity; focus on "Founding Team" roles. |
| **Series B** | High cash to compete with Big Tech. | Shift to share counts; equity becomes a "bonus" component. |

### **3. Equity, Bonus, & Benefits Policy**

#### **Equity Refresh Policy**
To prevent "equity cliff" attrition, the company will implement a **Performance-Based Refresh Program**.
*   **Eligibility:** Employees with 2+ years of tenure and "Exceeds Expectations" ratings.
*   **Mechanism:** Annual grants representing 10–25% of the original grant value, vesting over 4 years.

#### **Bonus Structure**
*   **Individual Contributors (Eng/Product/Ops):** 10–15% target based on OKR achievement.
*   **Sales (SDR/BDR):** 50/50 split (50% Base / 50% Variable) based on quota attainment.
*   **Leadership (VP/Head of):** 20–30% target based on company revenue and EBITDA milestones.

#### **Benefits Package Comparison**
| Benefit | Standard Startup (Seed) | Top-Tier AI Startup (Series B) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Health/Dental/Vision** | Basic PPO/HMO | Premium PPO + HSA Contribution |
| **401(k)** | Not offered | 401(k) with 3–4% Match |
| **PTO** | Unlimited (Unstructured) | Unlimited + Mandatory 3-day "Recharge" weeks |
| **Parental Leave** | 8–12 weeks (unpaid/standard) | 16–20 weeks (fully paid) |
| **Learning/Dev** | $500/year stipend | $3,000/year + Conference travel |
