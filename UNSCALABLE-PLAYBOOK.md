### Summary Strategy for the President:
Because you are a **501c3**, your "Sales" is actually **"Impact Alignment."** 

Don't build a complex dashboard yet. Build a **"High-Touch Digitization Service"** where the customer feels like they are working with a partner, not a software. The moment they feel they are "using a tool," you have lost the opportunity to provide the "white-glove" value that justifies your involvement. 

**Automate only when the manual labor prevents you from helping the next customer.**
