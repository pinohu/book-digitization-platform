### 3. QBR TEMPLATE (Quarterly Business Review)
*Focus: Connecting the technology to the mission of preserving Catholic Classics.*

* **I. Executive Summary:** Mission alignment (How many years of heritage were secured this quarter?).
* **II. Value Delivered:** 
    * Total books digitized.
    * Accuracy rate of AI vs. manual proofreading.
    * Estimated "Time Saved" for Criterion staff.
* **III. Usage Metrics:** Volume trends, upload frequency, and error/correction rates.
* **IV. ROI Calculation:** Cost of manual transcription vs. $5/book AI digitization.
* **V. Roadmap Preview:** Upcoming AI features (e.g., automated translation, better Latin/Greek character recognition).
* **VI. Expansion Opportunities:** Discussing the next batch of books or additional series.
* **VII. Feedback Loop:** "What is the biggest friction point in your scanning process?"

### 4. EXPANSION STRATEGY
*Goal: Net Retention >120% by increasing the volume of books processed.*

* **Upsell Triggers:**
    * **Volume Limits:** Criterion hits their quarterly target early $\rightarrow$ Offer a "Bulk Pre-pay" discount for the next 500 books.
    * **Team Growth:** Criterion hires more archivists $\rightarrow$ Offer "Multi-user Management" features.
    * **Feature Requests:** They need better searchability $\rightarrow$ Offer "Advanced Metadata/Indexing" as a premium add-on.
* **Cross-sell Identification:**
    * **Formatting Services:** Offer to convert digitized text into EPUB/MOBI for Kindle/E-readers.
    * **Translation Layer:** Use AI to offer English $\rightarrow$ Yoruba/Igbo/Hausa translations of the classics.
* **Target:** Aim for a 20% increase in book volume per quarter.

### 5. CHURN RISK SIGNALS & PLAYBOOKS

| Signal | Definition | Early Intervention Playbook |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Volume Drop** | 30% decrease in books uploaded MoM | **The "Workflow Audit":** Schedule a call to see if their physical scanning process is broken. |
| **The "Silent" Client** | No logins and no uploads for 21 days | **The "Value Check":** Send a "Miss you" email highlighting the books currently sitting in their physical backlog. |
| **High Error Rate** | Constant support tickets regarding AI accuracy | **The "Technical Deep Dive":** Offer a free training session on how to improve scan quality. |
| **Personnel Change** | Key contact at Criterion leaves | **The "New Stakeholder" Protocol:** Immediate outreach to the new contact to re-onboard them. |
| **Payment Friction** | Credit card declines or late transfers | **The "Financial Grace" Playbook:** Offer a temporary grace period or a different billing method (Wire vs. Card). |
| **Competitor Mention** | Asking about "other scanning services" | **The "Value Reinforcement":** Send a summary of their unique data security and mission alignment. |
| **Decreased Feature Use** | Using upload but ignoring metadata tools | **The "Feature Spotlight":** Send a 1-minute video showing how metadata makes their library searchable. |
| **Negative NPS** | Score of 0-6 | **The "Recovery Call":** President of Origin Eyes calls the President of Criterion personally. |
