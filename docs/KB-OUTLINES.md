# Content Strategy & Knowledge Base — Book Digitization Platform

> Dynasty Empire Content Plan | April 19, 2026

## Content Strategy Overview
The content strategy follows a "Scholar-First" approach, targeting the intersection of Catholic Theology and Digital Humanities. The primary keyword cluster is "Catholic Classic Digitization" and "RAG for Theological Research." The goal is to establish the platform as the gold standard for verbatim digital archives of sacred texts, moving the needle from generic OCR to "High-Fidelity Theological Preservation."
